Our fresh cow milk is sourced directly from local farms and delivered to your doorstep without any processing or additives. It's rich in calcium, protein, and essential nutrients that are vital for your health. Our cows are grass-fed and raised in a stress-free environment, ensuring the highest quality milk.

100% Pure and Natural
No Preservatives
Farm Fresh Daily
Rich in Nutrients

Our fresh cow milk is collected daily from healthy cows raised on local farms. We ensure that the milk is handled with utmost care to maintain its freshness and nutritional value. The milk is not processed or homogenized, preserving its natural goodness.

Each batch of milk is tested for quality and purity before delivery. We follow strict hygiene standards throughout the collection and delivery process to ensure that you receive only the best quality milk.

Our fresh cow milk is packed with essential nutrients:

  • Calcium: Essential for strong bones and teeth
  • Protein: Important for muscle growth and repair
  • Vitamin D: Helps in calcium absorption
  • Vitamin B12: Important for nerve function and the production of red blood cells
  • Potassium: Helps maintain healthy blood pressure
  • Phosphorus: Works with calcium to build strong bones

To maintain the freshness and quality of our milk:

  • Store the milk in the refrigerator at 4°C or below
  • Consume within 2 days of delivery for best taste and freshness
  • Do not leave the milk at room temperature for more than 2 hours
  • Always use a clean spoon to take out milk
  • Do not return unused milk to the original container
  • Freeze the milk if you want to store it for longer periods (up to 3 months)
